MARCO SIMONE ROME ITALY

SEPTEMBER 29 - OCTOBER 1, 2023
Marco Simone Golf & Country Club
The Italian capital city of Rome will host
the Ryder Cup for the first time in 2023.
The Marco Simone Golf and Country Club,
only 17km from the centre of Rome, will
follow in the footsteps of Club de Golf
Valderrama in Spain (1997) and Le Golf
National in France (2018) - when the 44th
edition of the match between Europe and
the United States is staged in the autumn
of 2023.
